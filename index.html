About Me

The person behind the blocks

Olivares is a Minecraft Bedrock studio specializing in building immersive worlds and powerful add-ons for the official Minecraft Marketplace.

Olivares has collaborated with top studios including Monster Egg Studios, Eescal Studios, Kubo Studios, 4KS Studios, Yeggs, Architect Edge, Pixelusion, Miracles Studios, Lua Studios, Pickaxe Studios, and more to deliver high-quality content enjoyed by millions of players worldwide.

From thrilling boss battles and parkour adventures to magical add-ons with unique mechanics — Olivares crafts experiences that stand out on the official Marketplace.
